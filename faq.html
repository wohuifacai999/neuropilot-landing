Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Who is NeuroPilot for?

NeuroPilot is for creators, side-hustlers, indie founders, and anyone ready to move from planning to doing. If you’ve ever struggled to start or stay consistent, it’s for you.

❓ Do I need to know how to code or use AI?

Nope. If you can type, you can use NeuroPilot. It’s designed to guide and assist, not confuse.

❓ What makes NeuroPilot different from other AI tools?

Most tools answer questions. NeuroPilot walks with you—remembers your goals, adapts your plan, and keeps momentum alive like a real partner.

❓ Is this just ChatGPT with a wrapper?

No. NeuroPilot combines memory, planning logic, execution tracking, and goal-oriented behavior into a purpose-built system for solo operators. It's not just a chatbot—it’s your strategy and operations assistant.

❓ Is it free?

We’re currently offering early access to a private beta for free. In the future, there may be subscription plans, but our early users will receive exclusive benefits.

❓ Can I suggest features or give feedback?

Absolutely. Early users help shape NeuroPilot’s future. Your input is not only welcome—it’s essential.
